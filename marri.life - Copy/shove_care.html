What is Shove.Care?

A few key topics covered by Shove.Care:

Plastics & Oils
Covers the lacking and fraudulent recycling industry as well as the impact of plastics and oils on the environment.
Water
Covers unsustainable water use practices that lead to situations like the Colorado River, which is on track to be dry by 2040.
Environmental Justics
Covers the unjust dumbing of chemicals and the higher density of large-scale environmental mishandlings that disadvantaged communities have been subject to.
Energy
Covers the effects of unsustainable methods of "creating" electricity and infefficent use of it.
Ecology
Covers the effects on biodiversity of the common lawn and the associated upkeep, and what that lack of biodiversity leads to.
